=====[QUEUES]===========================================

- A QUEUE IS AN ORDERED LIST WHICH USES A 
FIFO (FIRST IN FIRST OUT) APPROACH FOR 
ACCESSING ELEMENTS.

- INSERT OPERATIONS ARE PERFORMED AT THE REAR 
END WHILE DELETE OPERATIONS ARE PERFORMED 
AT THE FRONT END.

ENQUEUE => INSERTING
DEQUEUE => DELETING
